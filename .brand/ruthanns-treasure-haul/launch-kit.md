## $0 Organic Growth Plan (since we're skipping ads)

The goal for the first 30 days: **build the page audience without spending**.

1. **Cross-promo from existing pages** (already in the kit above) — instant 100-500 organic followers from existing Tolley audience.
2. **Tag the Page in Marketplace listings** — when Ruthann lists on her personal Marketplace, mention "Follow @RuthannsTreasureHaul on FB for new haul drops" in the description.
3. **Local FB groups** — share pieces (with link to tolley.io/shop, NOT links to the page directly, since most groups block self-promo) into:
   - Kansas City Buy/Sell/Trade
   - Independence MO Yard Sales
   - KC Vintage Lovers
   - Estate Sale Finds — Kansas City
4. **Facebook Stories** — every haul day, post 3-5 stories of new finds. Stories show up in followers' feeds even when posts get throttled.
5. **Cross-post to Ruthann's Kitchen audience** — soft mention on `tolley.io/food` newsletter ("Ruthann also flips vintage finds — check out tolley.io/shop").
6. **Reels every 7-10 days** — short 15-30s clips of "today's haul" or "before/after." FB algorithm boosts new-format video for small Pages.
7. **Engage in comments on competitor pages** — local KC vintage stores, estate sale companies. Genuine comments, no link spam.

**30-day target:** 200-500 followers, 5-10 inbound DMs from the Page (vs. 0 today), and a measurable lift in tolley.io/shop daily uniques.

When/if we want to switch on paid ads later, the page is already warm and we have audience data for retargeting/lookalikes.
